Erase a Joint or Member

To erase a joint or member from your structural model:

  1. Click the on the
  2. Click on the joint or member you want to erase.

Notes and Tips

You must be in the Drawing Board Mode to erase a joint or member.

You can only erase one joint or member at a time.

When you erase a joint, all attached to that joint are also deleted.

You cannot erase the that were created automatically by the Bridge Designer 2016 when you started a new design.

When you erase a member, all members with higher are re-numbered to fill the gap.

You can also delete a joint or delete a member to remove them from the .

If you accidentally erase a joint or member, click the to restore it.
