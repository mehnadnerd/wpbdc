Notes and Tips

The following actions can be undone:

  • draw a joint
  • draw a member
  • move a joint
  • change the properties of a member
  • delete or erase a joint
  • delete or erase a member

Any action that can be undone can also be restored using redo.

The Bridge Designer 2016 has "three-level undo." That is, you can only undo the three most recent changes to your structural model.
