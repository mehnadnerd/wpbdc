View Member List button

 Click the View Member List button to display or hide the Member List. This button functions as a "toggle." If the Member List is currently visible, clicking the button will hide it. If the Member List is hidden, clicking the button will display it.

Notes and Tips

The View Member List button is located on the Display Toolbar. It can also be accessed from the View menu.

The Member List can also be hidden from view by dragging it to the right-hand edge of the Drawing Board.
