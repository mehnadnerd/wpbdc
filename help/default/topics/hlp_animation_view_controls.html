Animation View Controls

The Animation View Controls move and rotate the viewpoint of the load test animation within the scene, creating the impression of flying under, over, and through the bridge.  They are normally shown in faded colors on the left side of the animation screen as shown here.  When you pass the mouse over any of the controls, it will brighten to full color.
Click and drag the Walk Control to move forward and turn left or right in the scene as though you are walking. Dragging upward moves forward. Dragging downward moves to the rear.
Click and drag the Slide Control to move laterally in any direction as though your are stepping sideways or moving up or down in an elevator within the scene.
Click and drag the Head Tilt Control to change the view as though tilting your head up, down, left, or right while observing the scene. For example, walk under a bridge span and tilt up to observe the structure beneath the deck.
Click the Home Control to move quickly back to a position where the whole bridge is visible.
Click and drag the Truck View Control to see what the truck driver sees as she drives over the bridge. Drag the mouse left, right, up, and down to see all around you.
