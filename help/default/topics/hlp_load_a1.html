Load a Sample Design

The Bridge Designer 2016 includes a variety of sample designs, which you can open, modify, and . All of these are stable truss configurations, but none of them are optimized for minimum cost.

To load a sample design:

  1. Click the located in the File menu.
  2. Select a sample design, and click OK.. The sample design will be displayed on the Drawing Board.

Notes and Tips

You must be in the Drawing Board Mode to load a sample design.

If your current has not been saved, you will be prompted to save it before the sample design is loaded.
