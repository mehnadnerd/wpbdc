Increase Member Size button

 Click the Increase Member Size button to increase the of the currently selected member(s) to the next larger size.

Notes and Tips

The Increase Member Size button is located on the Member Properties Toolbar.

If more than one member is selected, clicking this button will increase the size of all selected , even if they are different sizes. For example, if you have selected a 50mm member, a 90mm member, and a 120mm member simultaneously, clicking the Increase Member Size button will change these members to 55mm, 100mm, and 130mm, respectively.

When you use the Increase Member Size button, the is updated to reflect the change.

When you use the Increase Member Size button, the other two (material and cross-section type) are not changed.
