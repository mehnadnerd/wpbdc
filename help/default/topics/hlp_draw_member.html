Once you have drawn the that will connect your truss together, you can draw the that make up the structure itself. Members must be drawn from joint to joint.

To draw a member:

  1. Select the from the
  2. Position the mouse pointer over a joint.
  3. Click the left mouse button, and hold it down.
  4. Drag the mouse pointer to another joint, and release the button. A new member will be created from the first joint to the second.

Notes and Tips

To draw a member, you must be in the Drawing Board Mode.

When you create a new member, the currently displayed in the are automatically assigned to the new member. These properties can be changed later. (See Change the Properties of a Member for more information.)
