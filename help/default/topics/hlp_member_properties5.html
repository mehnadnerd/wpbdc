Notes and Tips

The Member Properties lists are located on the Member Properties Toolbar.

When adding new members, the material, cross-section, and size currently displayed in the three Member Properties lists are automatically assigned to each new member as it is created.

When editing the structural model, the Member Properties lists are used to change the properties of the currently selected member(s).
