Print the Load Test Results

To print a report of your most recent load test:

  1. Ensure that the printer is connected and on line.
  2. Click the on the Status Toolbar. The Load Test Results Report will be shown as a table in a separate window.
  3. Click the Print button in the right-hand side of the window. The Printer Selection Dialog will appear. You can select the desired printer and change other setting such as margin sizes. When finished, press Print to print the report.

Notes and Tips

The Load Test Results Report can also be copied to the Windows Clipboard by clicking the Copy button on the right-hand side of the window. The data are copied in tab-delimited text format. In this format, they can be pasted directly into a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet or a Word or Notepad document.
